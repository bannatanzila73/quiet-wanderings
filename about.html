About

This is Banna Tanzila.

A student from Tokyo International University, but this little corner of the internet isn't about that. Quiet Wanderings is where I keep the slower parts of my life — the hikes, the sunsets, the mornings that don't start with a to-do list.

I started keeping track of these moments because I noticed I was rushing through most of my days without really looking at them. Writing them down, even briefly, made me pay attention again.

If you're here, I hope something on this page makes you want to slow down for a minute too.
