Chasing the peaks

Somewhere in the Alps, standing in a field of wildflowers with a pack that felt heavier by the hour. My legs were done long before my head was ready to turn back. There's a kind of quiet you only get standing in front of something that big — no signal, no noise, just glaciers and grass and the sound of my own breathing.
